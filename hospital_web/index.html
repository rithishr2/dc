Introduction

A Hospital Management System (HMS) is a software that helps hospitals run smoothly by organizing and automating tasks like managing patient information, scheduling appointments, handling billing, tracking medications, and more. It's designed to make the work easier for doctors, nurses, staff, and hospital managers, and improve the quality of care for patients.

About Us

USES:

Patient Registration: Registers patient details quickly and accurately. Appointment Scheduling: Allows patients to book appointments with doctors. Doctor's Prescriptions: Doctors enter prescriptions directly into the system. Billing and Payments: Generates bills for services and supports online payments. Lab Test Management: Schedules tests and stores results for easy access. Staff Scheduling: Manages staff shifts and tracks attendance.
